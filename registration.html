Registration Guidelines

The following registration guidelines are to be followed:

  • Registration by at least one of the paper's authors is required for participation
  • Presentation of the paper at the event is mandatory, to ensure it's inclusion in the conference proceedings
  • Guest attendees must also be registered (as non-author)
