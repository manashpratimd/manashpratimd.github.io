About the ICSDEC Conference


International Conference on Sustainable Development in Electronics and Computing is a research conference organised by Assam Engineering College and Guahati University. The primary vision of the conference is to help bridge the gap between sustainability and innovations.

The goal of this conference is to bring together researchers from around the world to share new ideas, research results and their enlightening experience in various fields. We warmly welcome all the authors to submit your research papers to ICSDEC.
