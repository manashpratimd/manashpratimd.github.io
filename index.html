International Conference on Sustainable Development in Electronics and Computing

With special focus on Digital Platforms and Semiconductors

18-19 October | Guwahati

About

CoSDEC

International Conference on Sustainable Development in Electronics and Computing is a research conference organised by Department of Electronics and TeleCommunications Engineering & Computer Science Engineering of Assam Engineering College jointly with the Department of Electronics and Communications Engineering of Gauhati University. The primary vision of the conference is to help bridge the gap between sustainability and innovations.

Objectives

  • To contribute to the development, progress and welfare of societies (S/Y/W) by disseminating technological knowledge and experience.
  • To provide a prestigious international forum for specialist presentations and interactions in one or more areas of Engineering and Technology with in its tracks through papers, exhibitions, plenary talk, tutorials, etc.
  • To encourage the study and discussion of technology and technological applications in a broad social, political and human context.

Assam Engineering College

Assam Engineering College, the edifice that stands tall with dignity because of the plethora of engineers that has graduated from its mainspring, since its foundation in 1955, has been the premier institute in North East, regarding this field and has been celebrated for the same. The college provides Bachelor degrees (B-TECH) in eight fields mainly- Mechanical, Civil, Electronics and Telecommunications, Electrical, Chemical, Instrumentation, Computer Science & Engineering and Industrial & Production.
