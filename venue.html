Assam Engineering College

ASSAM ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Assam Engineering College, the edifice that stands tall with dignity because of the plethora of engineers that has graduated from its mainspring, since its foundation in 1955, has been the premier institute in North East, regarding this field and has been celebrated for the same. The college is situated away from the clamor of the capital city Guwahati, and is nestled in the lap of nature surrounded by hills and colorful flame trees extended to several acres of land. It also provides Masters in Mechanical, Electrical and Civil Engineering, and Master of Computer Applications.

Gauhati University

GAUHATI UNIVERSITY

Gauhati University is a prestigious institution of higher education located in Guwahati, Assam, India. Established in 1948, it is one of the oldest and largest universities in the Northeastern region of India. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across a wide range of disciplines including arts, science, commerce, management, law, engineering, technology, and social sciences.
