SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Authors are invited to submit their formatted, complete papers. All submission will be double-blind peer reviewed and evalulated based on relevance, originality, content and correctness.

REVIEW PROCESS

The paper selection will follow a double-blind review process. Therefore all authors MUST adhere to the following guidelines to conceal their identity:
1: Eliminate author names, contact information, and affiliations from the entire manuscript (including metadata of the document)
2: Cite all relevant prior work (including your own) in third person (for example, "it has been shown that ...[1]; do not use words like "my" or "our")
3: Eliminate acknowledgements and references to funding sources. These can be added later if the paper is accepted.
The review process will include a software-based plagiarism check.
